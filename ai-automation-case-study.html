Prompt Structure Example

Role: Act as a Business Analyst and QA documentation partner. Context: Review the provided workflow and identify process gaps, validation risks, and improvement opportunities. Task: Generate a structured implementation plan with assumptions, risks, dependencies, and acceptance criteria. Output: Return a concise plan with sections for business need, technical approach, validation rules, and next steps.

Lessons Learned

Key Insight

AI productivity improves most when paired with clear process thinking, strong instructions, human review, and repeatable workflow templates. The strongest results come from treating AI as a structured assistant inside a governed workflow rather than as a replacement for analysis or judgment.
